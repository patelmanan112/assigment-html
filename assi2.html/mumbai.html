Mumbai

Mumbai, India’s financial capital, is more than just a city — it’s an emotion. Known as the ‘City of Dreams’, Mumbai is where millions come with hopes, and the city welcomes everyone with open arms.

From the iconic Gateway of India and Marine Drive to the busy lanes of Colaba and the glamour of Bollywood, Mumbai is a blend of history, culture, and modern life. The city never sleeps — whether it’s the buzzing local trains, the aroma of street food like vada pav, or the peaceful sunsets at Juhu Beach.
